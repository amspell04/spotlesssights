About Us

Welcome to Spotless Sights, your trusted local window cleaning company, owned and operated by two longtime friends, Alex Ray and Alex Xiao. Currently pursuing degrees in accounting and business administration, Alex Ray and Alex Xiao bring a fresh, entrepreneurial spirit to Spotless Sights. Their academic backgrounds equip them with the latest industry insights and innovative techniques, ensuring that every job is completed with the highest standards of professionalism and efficiency. Their business, Spotless Sights, is committed to not only delivering sparkling clean windows but also to fostering genuine connections with their clients. Welcome to Spotless Sights, where clarity meets perfection, and your satisfaction is always their priority!
